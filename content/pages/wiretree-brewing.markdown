##At Home Brewing with Adrian and Kevin


In 2013, Adrian Ogletree and Kevin Wierman started brewing beer from home as a hobby.
The original setup used 2 gallon stock pots to boil the wort and the snow outside to cool the wort before pitching.

Step-by-step the apparatus has been growing to include more sophisticated methods of controlling brewing processes, and to allow more exotic recipes to be tried and tested. Kevin thoroughly believes in building as much by hand as possible in order to maintain a working knowledge of the apparatus and an air of homebrew authenticity.

As the old adage goes, "Three hours in the Library saves three months in the lab." So, Adrian has undertaken the rather daunting task of reaching out to beer-oriented review magazines in hope of quantifying the data we will need to become the brewers we hope to be one day.

Adrian and Kevin have become ardent brewing enthusiasts and spend much of their spare time supporting the local brewing communities. They hope to grow out own operation into something more people can enjoy.
