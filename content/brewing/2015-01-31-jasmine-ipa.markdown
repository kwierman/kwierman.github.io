## Background

One of our favorite beers of all time is the [Avatar Jasmine IPA](http://www.elysianbrewing.com/elysian-beers/avatar-jasmine-ipa/)from [Elysian Brewing](http://www.elysianbrewing.com/). So much so, in fact, that we're going to try and replicate it from a series of other recipes. We've tried this before (to great success, might I add). This time around, we're making some modifications to the now named,

{% blockquote %}
	Wrath of Khan Jasmine IPA
{% endblockquote%}


## Desired Characteristics

From the Elysian Brewing website:

* Style:
	* IPA
* Body:
	* Medium
* Color
	* Golden Copper
	* Roughly an SRM of 6
* ABV:
	* 6.3$$\%$$
* Aromatics:
	* Floral
	* Jasmine
* Taste:
	* Balance of hoppiness and jasmine
* IBU:
	* 45

Malts: Pale, $$45^\circ$$ Crystal, Munich and Cara-hell

Hops: Bittered with German Northern Brewer, finished with Glacier and Amarillo hops
