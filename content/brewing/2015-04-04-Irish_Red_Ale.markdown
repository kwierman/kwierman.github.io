Title: Irish Red Ale
Date: 2015-04-04
Categories: brewing


Today we're making an Irish Red Ale. The goal here is to hop in the direction of a floral tea. The body should be lightly sweet with a dry finish. The color should be red.

In order to get the right hoppiness, we're going to go with Kent Goldings.

For an economical ale, we're using 2-Row for the base malt. For a sweet, dry body with red color, we're using Caramel 20L and 120L.
