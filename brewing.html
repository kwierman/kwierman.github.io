WireTree Brewing

Aug 4th, 2014

In 2013, Adrian Ogletree and I started brewing beer from home as a hobby. Since then, we’ve developed our little brewing operation into a larger scale production, adding more techniques to our repertoire. Additionally, we’ve been performing research into brewing trends across the country using data provided by beer review magazines [citations pending].

The Dream

We’re investigating what we could accomplish if we invested our research into a commercial brewery. Walk into a brewery anywhere and ask to see their menu. In any given season, breweries will offer a selection of beers indicative of the local preferences, and the season in which they were brewed.

In many cases the selection will follow a narrow trend. Think of every time you’ve walked into a bar and seen ten different IPAs. We’re going to change that.
