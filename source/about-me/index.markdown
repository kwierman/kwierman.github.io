<h2>Who am I?</h2>
<p>
I am Kevin Wierman, an experimental nuclear physicist, a coder, and a brewer.
</p>
<p>
My mission is to bring modern design practices to the fields of physics and brewing. Currently, I am working on the <a href="http://www.katri\
n.kit.edu/">KATRIN</a> collaboration to measure the neutrino mass. I participated in constructing and commissioning the <a href="http://arxiv.org/abs\
/1404.2925">KATRIN Focal Plane Detector (FPD)</a> system. Cheifly, I designed and implemented the FPD Cosmic Ray Veto. At the moment, I am working wi\
th the Katrin Electron Gun comissioning.
</p>
